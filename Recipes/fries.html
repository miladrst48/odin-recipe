french fries



Description

cut the potatoes very thin and then put one batch of them in hot oil

Ingredients
  • potatoes
  • oil
  • salt
Steps
  1. skin the potatoes
  2. cut them in small thin pieces
  3. heat oil in a pan
  4. pour salt on the potatoes and sort thrm in the pan
  5. let them fry till their crispy
  6. enjoy
